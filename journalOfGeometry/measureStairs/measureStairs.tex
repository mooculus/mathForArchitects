\begin{question}
Read this \link[\textit{ArchDaily}
  article]{https://www.archdaily.com/892647/how-to-make-calculations-for-staircase-designs},
and define the terms:
\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{steps}
\item \textbf{tread}
\item \textbf{riser}
\item \textbf{nosing}
\item \textbf{steep and low-transit staircase}
\item \textbf{optimal staircase}
\item \textbf{loose staircase}
\end{enumerate}
Use words and pictures in your definitions as needed/helpful.

\end{question}

    staircases.
